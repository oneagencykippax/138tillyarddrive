138 Tillyard Drive, Flynn

Immaculate, centrally located and boasting plenty of extra features, this Flynn home is a must-see for anyone needing space, privacy and convenience.

The floorplan features four bedrooms, all with built-in robes and an ensuite for the master, plus a studio with ensuite in the garage.

For the home cook, the spacious kitchen has plenty of bench and storage space, plus gas cooking, a 900mm wide oven and breakfast bar for casual dining.

When it's time to relax, there is a large living room, a side patio for entertaining friends, a barbecue alfresco area and native gardens with a pond and fruits trees where you can wander and be surrounded by nature.

Built for the climate, this property features ducted heating and cooling and double glazed windows in the main areas.

The garage is sure to impress, with space for more than 6 cars, a workshop and studio with ensuite, the man of the house will be otherwise occupied for the foreseeable future.

Additional features include a garden shed for extra storage, extensive landscaping and off-street parking for guests, caravans or boats.

All of this is set on a 1046m2 fully-fenced block close to local schools, shops and public transport.

Onsite Auction 11am 04/02/2017, this home MUST be sold on the day.
